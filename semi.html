    Semi Husked Coconut

    A semi-husked coconut is a versatile and partially processed coconut that retains a portion of its natural fibrous husk. Ideal for various culinary and non-culinary uses, it offers a balance between ease of use and natural freshness.

    • Rich in Nutrients: Provides dietary fiber, essential vitamins (like B vitamins and vitamin C), and important minerals (such as potassium and magnesium).
    • Healthy Fats: Contains medium-chain triglycerides (MCTs) that support energy and metabolic health.
    • Hydration: Coconut water helps maintain fluid balance and is naturally hydrating.
